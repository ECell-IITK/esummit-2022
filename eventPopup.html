Be An Angel

Pitching your idea to a group of investors is incredibly nerve-wracking.You wonder if they’ll like your idea. You wonder if they’ll like you. Most of all, one wonders if they’re going to fund you or not. Even if one has money in the bank, there’s going to come a point when one simply has to get additional funding to grow their startup and start scaling up. So we bring to you a competition that will actually put you in the shoes of investors thereby teaching you a lot about what investors want and how to position the companies for successful pitches in the future. Students participating here are the investors, not the entrepreneurs. They do not have to come with startup ideas. Rather, they would play the role of Angel Investors/Analysts in a VC firm assessing the startups being posed to them as potential investments.
